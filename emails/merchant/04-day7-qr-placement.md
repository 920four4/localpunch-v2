Week one — done. Let's talk about your QR code placement, because this is the difference between 2 signups a day and 20.

### 3 places that work

**1. Right at the card reader.**
Best spot. They're already looking. Their phone is out. Just stick it there.

**2. On the receipt / bag.**
Print a small version. They see it at home, on the desk, reminds them next visit.

**3. The "while you wait" spot.**
Counter where people wait for their food/coffee/whatever. Bored eyes = scan ready.

### 1 place it doesn't work

**The door.** Feels like it should. Doesn't. People walking in are on a mission. People walking out are fleeing. Nobody stops at doors.

### How big?

For a countertop display: 4x4 inches minimum. Smaller than that and phones struggle to focus.

For a wall (behind the bar, etc): 8x8 inches. Has to be readable from 6 feet away.
