### The script (memorize it)

> "We started a card — get 10 punches, get [your reward]. Want me to set you up? Takes 30 seconds, just need your phone."

That's it. Don't explain the app. Don't say "loyalty program" (people tune out). Just say what they *get*.

### When to say it

- At checkout, right when the total comes up
- Before they put their phone away
- Not while they're ordering (they're deciding)

### If they say no

> "No worries — the QR's right there if you change your mind."

Don't push. The QR on the counter will pick up the stragglers.

### What to avoid

- **Don't** email the QR. People don't scan QRs from their own email.
- **Don't** explain the 60-second cooldown unless they ask.
- **Don't** hand them a paper card "for now" — you'll confuse your own punches.

10 signups. That's it. That's the number that tells you whether you'll still be using LocalPunch in 6 months, because after 10 people are walking around with a card, the program runs itself.
