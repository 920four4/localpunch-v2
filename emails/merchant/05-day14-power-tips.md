### Tip 1: Check the dashboard on Mondays

Not for vanity metrics. For this one question:
> Who's close to a reward but hasn't come in this week?

You can see it on your dashboard — people with 8 or 9 punches who haven't been in for 5+ days. They're your easy wins. A simple "hey, you're one punch from a free coffee" text or mention next time they come in brings most of them back.

### Tip 2: Make redemption feel like winning

When someone hits their reward, *react*. Announce it. Slip a note in with it. Whatever fits your shop. The moment they get the free coffee is the moment they tell their friends about LocalPunch. Make it memorable.

People who have their 10-punch moment celebrated come back and start a second card. That's where the real magic is.
